History of the Mossage
Got Stress?
Let me get you Moore Relaxed in dealing with life's most difficult endeavors. Allow yourself the time in these stress filled days to take care of yourself, treat yourself, and relax. My knowledge of muscles and superb massaging techniques will help your body maintain wellness so you can enjoy life!

All of Mossage Health's business is conducted in the most nurturing, ethical, compassionate, and responsible manner!

I started my practice in 2007 at beautiful southern California! For those ten years since then, I have only worked for chiropractors and physical therapists.

While working with these professionals, I was able to learn really cool stuff, like laser therapy, and ultrasound electric stem. I also gained tons of experience with pre, and post, operative clients.

A good amount of the chiropractors I worked with also had an abundance of prefessional athletes: cyclists, skaters, BMX bikers, a California state champion bodybuilder, and I was even able to work on two Olympic gold medalist gymnasts at the time!

I like being on the front lines of physical activities, where people really need me. On my off days, I helped runners and road-bike racers with their pre-race warmup stretches, and cool-down massages (to reduce all that lactic acid buildup. They really appreciated it, and so did the people I worked for (since I brought them more business)!

Moving to michigan has provided me with a great opportunity to show a new set of people the skills that I have gained, and how well I can use them! It really makes my day when I can help anyone start the process of living a pain free or reduced pain lifestyle!
